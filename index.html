APPLE

An apple is a popular, round, edible fruit from the Malus domestica tree, known for being sweet, crisp, and packed with fiber, Vitamin C, and antioxidants, eaten fresh or cooked into many dishes like pies, sauces, and cider, with thousands of varieties grown globally for diverse tastes and uses. Botanically, it's a pome, with its edible flesh developing from the flower's receptacle rather than just the ovary.

MANGO

Mango is a sweet, tropical fruit from the Mangifera indica tree, native to South Asia and widely cultivated in tropical regions. It is known for its sweet, juicy, orange-yellow flesh, and varieties differ in size, shape, and color. The fruit is rich in vitamins A and C, fiber, and various minerals, and it can be eaten fresh, dried, or used in dishes like salsas, chutneys, and curries.
